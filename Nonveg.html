Chicken Chilli

"Besan ki Sabzi is a delicious and wholesome Indian dish made with gram flour, tempered with aromatic spices, and cooked to a creamy, velvety consistency. Its earthy flavors and rustic appeal make it a comforting choice, pairing beautifully with roti or rice for a satisfying homemade meal."

Ingrediants

For Besan Ki Wadi

½ cup Curd, beaten, दही
½ tsp Turmeric powder, हल्दी पाउडर
½ tsp Degi red chilli powder, देगी लाल मिर्च पाउडर
A pinch of asafoetida, हींग
1 cup Gram flour, बेसन
Salt to taste, नमक स्वादानुसार
1 tsp Oil, तेल

For Curd Mixture

2 cups Curd, beaten, दही
1 tsp Ghee, घी
Salt to taste, नमक स्वादानुसार
2 Green chilli, chopped, हरी मिर्च
1 inch Ginger, chopped, अदरक
1 tsp Turmeric powder, हल्दी पाउडर
½ tsp Degi red chilli powder, देगी लाल मिर्च पाउडर
A pinch of asafoetida, हींग
1 ½ tbsp Coriander powder, धनिया पाउडर
1 tsp Gram flour, बेसन

For Gravy

2 tsp Ghee, घी
¼ tsp Fenugreek seeds, मेथी दाना
½ tsp Cumin seeds, जीरा
½ tsp Fennel seeds, सौंफ
2-3 Dry red chillies, सूखी लाल मिर्च
A pinch of asafoetida, हींग
Prepared Curd Mixture, तैयार किया हुआ दही का मिश्रण
1 cup Water, पानी
Prepared Besan Wadi, तैयार किया हुआ बेसन वडी

For Tadka

1 tsp Oil, तेल
¼ tsp Carom seeds, अजवाइन
¼ tsp Onion seeds, कलौंजी
¼ tsp White sesame seeds, सफेद तिल
A pinch of degi red chilli powder, देगी लाल मिर्च पाउडर

Other Ingredients

Oil for greasing, तेल

For Garnish

fresh Mint leaves, पुदीने के पत्ते
